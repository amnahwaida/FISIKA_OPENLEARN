## 1. Postulat Einstein & Faktor Lorentz

Albert Einstein merumuskan dua postulat:
1. Hukum fisika sama di semua kerangka acuan inersia.
2. Kecepatan cahaya $c$ konstan bagi semua pengamat.

**Faktor Lorentz ($\gamma$):**

$$
\Large \gamma = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2}}}
$$

---

## 2. Dilatasi Waktu

$$
\Large \Delta t = \gamma \cdot \Delta t_0
$$

---

## 3. Kontraksi Panjang

$$
\Large L = \frac{L_0}{\gamma}
$$

---

## 4. Energi Relativistik

$$
\Large E = mc^2 = \gamma m_0 c^2
$$

**Energi Kinetik:**

$$
\Large E_k = (\gamma - 1) m_0 c^2
$$

---

## 5. Efek Fotolistrik

$$
\Large hf = hf_0 + E_k
$$

---

## 6. Efek Compton

$$
\Large \Delta \lambda = \frac{h}{mc} (1 - \cos \theta)
$$

---

## 7. Hipotesis De Broglie

$$
\Large \lambda = \frac{h}{mv}
$$

---

## 8. Ketidakpastian Heisenberg

$$
\Large \Delta x \cdot \Delta p \geq \frac{h}{4\pi}
$$

---

## 9. Radiasi Benda Hitam

$$
\Large E = n \cdot h \cdot f
$$
